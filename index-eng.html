Who are we?

We're young people in wheelchairs. We're not going to spend our lives in our apartments. We want to go to museums and theaters, clubs and movies, walk in parks, use public transport, including the subway.

What is the purpose?

The purpose of the project "access is allowed" is to make the lives of people with disabilities more accessible and comfortable in terms of movement through the streets of Moscow. Thanks to the map, you can easily and quickly reach any destination, as well as walk through the beautiful corners of the city.
